# Site Notice v4 — Workflow improvements

v4 builds on v3's multi-notice summary cards and addresses gaps identified in a planning officer walkthrough. Key changes:

- **Check your answers** page before sending (GDS best practice)
- **Context-aware card actions** — pending cards show "Confirm displayed" as primary action
- **Created date** shown on all cards for audit trail
- **Send reminder** link for notices awaiting confirmation
- **"Not required" captures a reason** for audit purposes
- **Specific button text** throughout ("Confirm display", not "Save")
- **Single variant** — full detail cards only (no A/B comparison)
